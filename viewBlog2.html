VMI continues to focus its clients on innovation, convergence and disruption to drive growth and market leadership. When you consider the acceleration and profound change, taking place in technology coupled with technology as a foundational enabler of industry, you understand why every enterprise is experiencing some level of disruption and why it’s going to continue.

No industry is immune to this disruption nor should they want to be. Disruption is the market’s way of shaking things up, getting rid of the dead wood and refreshing itself in preparation for the new challenges ahead. One of the most common traps that people fall into as they move from the stable environment of the past to the disruption mode of the future is to attempt to rely on their experience for success in the future. This is a fatal mistake because the experience of the past is not the road to success in the future and in fact it will prove to be a boat anchor. A big part of your success will be how well you adapt to risk as opposed to thinking about mitigation of risk. With disruption comes great opportunity and that is where we work with our clients to win new business and to grow.
